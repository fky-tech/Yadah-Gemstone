About Us

Making a Difference in Ethiopia

Yadah Gemstone PLC is a dynamic and forward-thinking company established in 2017 with a clear vision to make a significant difference in Ethiopia's vital agriculture and mining sectors.

Founded by a team of experienced professionals across diverse industries, our company is committed to sustainable growth, innovation, and contributing to the economic development of the nation.

Our mission is to be a leader in the mining and agriculture sectors, making a tangible positive impact on the Ethiopian economy through sustainable practices and superior quality products. We aim to diversify our portfolio and leverage our expertise in construction and water works to support broader national development goals.

Founding Team

Mining & Agriculture Specialists

Our founding team brings together decades of experience across diverse industries, with deep expertise in mining operations, agricultural development, and business management. Their combined knowledge and hands-on approach guide the company's commitment to innovation and excellence.

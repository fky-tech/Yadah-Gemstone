Our Agricultural Division

Yadah Gemstone's agricultural division manages a vast farm of **over 200 hectares** in the southwest region of Ethiopia. We specialize in the cultivation of a wide range of **export-quality** cereal crops and spices, contributing to both local food security and international trade.

We maintain a key collaboration with **TBA Trading PLC** to enhance our operations and market reach, ensuring our products meet the highest quality standards for both domestic and international markets.

Our Agricultural Products

We cultivate a diverse range of high-quality crops and spices for both domestic consumption and international export markets.

Sesame

Premium quality sesame seeds cultivated for both domestic use and international export markets.

Mung Beans

Kidney Beans

High-protein kidney beans carefully cultivated and processed for maximum nutritional value.

Our Vision for Mining

Yadah Gemstone PLC is a primary producer and exporter of high-quality minerals from our owned and operated mining sites across the Southern, Southwestern, and Eastern Oromia regions, as well as Dire Dawa.

Our operations are rooted in ethical and efficient extraction, ensuring a sustainable approach that benefits both our partners and the local communities. We are committed to processing all minerals to meet stringent export quality standards.

Our Minerals

We specialize in the ethical extraction and export of these valuable minerals, contributing to Ethiopia's economic growth while maintaining the highest environmental and quality standards.

Gemstone

Precious and semi-precious stones ethically sourced for high-end markets.

Gold

High-purity gold extracted using environmentally responsible methods and technology.

Copper (Cu)

Industrial-grade copper for a wide range of global manufacturing markets.

Coal

High-quality thermal and metallurgical coal for efficient energy production.

Lithium

An essential mineral for modern battery technology and renewable energy storage solutions.

Tantalum

Conflict-free tantalum for high-tech electronics and specialized applications.

Niobium

A specialty metal for high-strength steel alloys and advanced superconductor technology.
